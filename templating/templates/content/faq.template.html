FAQ

What is ArtSimily?

ArtSimily is a platform where you can rate the similarity of various pieces of digital art.

What do I do?

When you click on the play button, ArtSimily will show you pairs of art pieces which you can rate on a four point scale. After some questions, you will get a score telling you how well you did.

How is the score generated?

ArtSimily compares some of your answers to the answers of other players who were shown the same questions. The higher the agreement, the higher your score.

What do you mean by ‘similarity’?

We don’t have a clear definition, so it can mean whatever the majority of players think it means.

Where does the art come from?

When you hover over a piece, a text-box should appear which shows the source of this particular piece. All pieces were released under a creative commons license by their creators.

I had a piece without a source text-box, why is that?

A few of the pieces don’t show the attribution correctly. This is a bug and we are working on fixing it.

Why do you need to know the similarity of these things?

ArtSimily is a research project, trying to establish a basis dataset in order to improve the quality of results generated by multimedia search engines.

Who is behind ArtSimily?

ArtSimily is a project of the Databases and Information Systems Research Group at the University of Basel in Switzerland.

I have more questions, how do I get in touch?

You can contact us via Twitter: @ArtSimily or via email: negfvzvyl@negfvzvyl.pbz. We would love to hear from you.
